About Brewed Awakening

Brewed Awakening is here to help you kickstart your morning and get a good jump on the day.
We offer a wide variety of a caffinated selection as well as a decafinated selection. Brewed Awakening
started as coffee combinations in the kitchen, sharing with friends, and soon turned into sharing it with
the community so all can try our favorite coffee combos and add their own twist.
